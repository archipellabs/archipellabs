# TimberWorks

## Overview

**TimberWorks** is a company specialized in the distribution of **wood materials and construction components** for architectural and building projects.

The company offers a catalog structured around:

- raw wood materials
- construction components
- carpentry elements
- natural decorative materials
- construction kits

The positioning of TimberWorks is based on a simple idea:

> provide a coherent and specialized wood catalog rather than a generic marketplace.

The shop is used by different types of customers:

- builders and architects  
- collaborative project creators  
- community administrators  
- construction and design enthusiasts  

The project is loosely inspired by a well-known block-based construction universe, but the store is designed as a **realistic e-commerce website specialized in wood construction materials**.

---

# Positioning

Unlike large general marketplaces, TimberWorks follows a **vertical specialization approach**.

The catalog intentionally focuses on a single domain: **wood and wood-based materials**.

This approach provides:

- a more coherent catalog
- stronger product expertise
- simplified supply chain management
- a better user experience for builders

---

# Product Range

The catalog is structured around several main product families.

## Raw Wood

Base materials from different wood species:

- logs  
- wood blocks  
- stripped wood  
- raw timber  

These materials form the foundation of most construction projects.

---

## Processed Wood

Products derived from raw wood materials:

- planks  
- prepared wood  
- panels  
- modular wood materials  

They are used for structural construction and assembly.

---

## Construction Components

Prefabricated elements designed for architecture:

- stairs  
- slabs  
- fences  
- gates  
- doors  
- trapdoors  

These components allow builders to quickly create consistent structures.

---

## Signage

Display and identification elements:

- signs  
- hanging signs  
- wall-mounted signage  

These elements are commonly used to organize spaces or label areas.

---

## Natural Decorative Elements

Environmental materials used for decoration:

- leaves  
- roots  
- natural vegetation elements  

These components help integrate constructions into natural surroundings.

---

## Workshop and Storage

Utility items related to woodworking and logistics:

- chests  
- barrels  
- crafting stations  
- scaffolding  
- ladders  

These elements help organize workspaces and storage areas.

---

## Transport

Some products allow transportation of materials and resources:

- boats  
- storage boats with integrated chests  

---

# Construction Kits

In addition to individual components, TimberWorks offers **ready-to-build construction kits**.

These bundles group compatible elements together to simplify project creation.

Examples include:

- Cabin Starter Kit  
- Watchtower Kit  
- Tea House Kit  
- Dock Construction Kit  

These kits are especially popular among users who want to quickly start building projects.

---

# Supply Chain

TimberWorks does not directly manufacture the materials.

Instead, the company works with a network of **specialized suppliers**, each focusing on specific wood species or transformation processes.

## Raw Material Suppliers

These partners produce raw wood materials.

Examples:

- OakHeart Forestry  
- Northern Timber Co  
- Jungle Canopy Supply  
- Mangrove Delta Woods  
- Cherry Blossom Mills  

---

## Wood Processing Partners

These suppliers transform raw materials into construction components.

Typical products include:

- stairs  
- doors  
- fences  
- panels  

Main partner:

**Voxel Carpentry Works**

---

## Specialized Suppliers

Some rare or exotic materials come from specialized producers.

Example:

**Nether Growers Collective**
